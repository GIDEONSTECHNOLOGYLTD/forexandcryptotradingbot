Risk Management Tips

Start Conservative

Begin with lower limits and increase gradually as you gain confidence.

2% Rule

Never risk more than 2% of your total capital on a single trade.

Daily Loss Limit

Set to 5-10% of total capital to protect your account from major drawdowns.

Leverage Caution

Higher leverage = higher risk. Use 1-3x for safer, more sustainable trading.
